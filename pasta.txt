Ingredients:
For the Meat Sauce:

1 lb (450g) ground beef or sausage (or a mix of both)

1 onion, chopped

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes

1 can (6 oz) tomato paste

1 tbsp tomato sauce (optional for extra richness)

1 tsp dried oregano

1 tsp dried basil

Salt and pepper to taste

For the Ricotta Mixture:

15 oz ricotta cheese

1 egg

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (divided)

1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Other:

12 lasagna noodles (regular or no-boil)

Extra mozzarella cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions:
Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

Cook the noodles: If using regular lasagna noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside. (Skip this step if using no-boil noodles.)

Make the meat sauce:

In a large pan, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the ground beef or sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.

Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until softened.

Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce (if using), oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 20 minutes to let the flavors combine, stirring occasionally.

Prepare the ricotta mixture:

In a bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella. Add the parsley if using. Stir until well combined.

Assemble the lasagna:

Put inside bowl
