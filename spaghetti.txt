Ingredients:
100g spaghetti

a bit of something

2 tbsp olive oil

3 garlic cloves, minced

a bit of parsley

1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes

A pinch of red pepper flakes (optional)

Fresh basil or parsley (optional)

Grated Gouda cheese (optional)

Instructions:
Cook the Spaghetti: Boil a pot of salted water, then cook the spaghetti according to package instructions (about 8-10 minutes). Drain, reserving some pasta water.

Make the Sauce: While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Add Tomatoes: Pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using). Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.

Combine: Toss the cooked spaghetti into the sauce, adding a little reserved pasta water if needed to thin the sauce.

Serve: Top with fresh basil, parsley, and grated Parmesan if desired. Enjoy!
